Kingsborough Community College is a public community college in Manhattan Beach, Brooklyn, New york. It is a part of the City University of New York system, and is the only community college in Brooklyn.

You can choose from over 6o degree and certificate programs at Kingsborough.
You can take classes during the day, evening and weekends in person or online.

Kingsborough Community College is an Achieving the Dream Leader College, a national designation in recognition of our commitment to improving student succsess and closing achievement gaps.
